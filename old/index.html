Quanta

This project has Particle Photon at its core. The managing program is called Quanta and was first created on September 2018. Quanta was created with the purpose to be used to program a Particle Photon controlling an air heater plant, at the Laboratory of Marine Engineering,NTUA.

Optimization of small autonomous vessels' functionality in operational level

[From the abstract]
In this paper, proceedings of small autonomous vessels integration in today’s maritime industry are discussed. Not to be omitted, the role of modern ports is of huge importance on that matter and will be presented thoroughly in the following paragraphs. In this era of automation and big data, sea operations with conventional ships are beginning to look a bit obsolete and without any future prospects. This could not apply more to rescue vessels where the minimization of operational errors is crucial in preventing human losses at sea. Thus, one algorithm of path planning will be presented addressing the huge delays that the human factor imposes in today’s sea rescue missions. Finally, useful diagrams will be produced underlining better practices regarding the choice of the port’s rescue ships and their operation.
